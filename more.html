Frontend Development as a Career

Frontend Development is simply the development side of everything associated with what the user sees and interacts with in the application or program being built. Kind of like the front end of the store. The customers see and interact with and purchase the products, but don't see the manufacturing or packing or hidden processes in the backend, hidden away from the customer view. Frontend development is all about making stuff pretty for people, and I'm super passionate about everything aesthetic there is.

How I Build These Projects.

I build these projects by utilising Scrimba, Google and Stack Overflow to aid me in learning various representational techniques using Javascript, HTML and CSS. Scrimba is a fantastic training resource and has probably the best educational training system I've ever seen in a computer science training course. The instructors are amazing, and so is the material and accessibility of training and learning.
